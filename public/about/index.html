Our Story

Founded in 2011, the IT Club of Jaffna Hindu College is dedicated to digitalizing school activities and promoting technology among students. We empower members through hands-on learning in coding, web and app development, robotics, and real-world problem-solving..

The club conducts coding workshops, hackathons, robotics projects, and training for competitions to build skills and inspire innovation. Today, it stands as a hub for students to explore, create, and lead in the world of technology.
