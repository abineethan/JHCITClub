Digital Literacy: A Journey Through ICT

About the Book

Digital Literacy: A Journey Through ICT, published by the IT Club of Jaffna Hindu College, is an insightful guide that explores the ever-evolving world of Information and Communication Technology. Covering key topics such as Engineering, AI Technology, Cybercrime, the Internet of Things (IoT), Cryptocurrency, and Robotics, this book aims to equip readers with essential knowledge and awareness of the digital age. Whether you are a student, educator, or tech enthusiast, this publication serves as a valuable resource to understand the opportunities and challenges of our interconnected world, inspiring the next generation to navigate and shape the future of ICT with confidence.
